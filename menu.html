Appetizers

Salmon Bites: Warm maple and ginger glaze, drizzled over bite sized pieces of roasted salmon, sprinkled with toasted sesame seeds.

Grilled Halumi: Mediterranean cheese imported from the island of Cyprus. Known for its slightly salty, feta-like consistency, served with fresh lemons and olive oil dill dressing.

Salsa Baked Goat Cheese: A goat cheese and cream cheese mixture covered with a roasted tomato and jalapeño salsa. Baked and served with crackers.

Moroccan Dip Trio: Sun-dried tomato hummus, muhummara*, and zatar dipping oil*. Served with warm pita bread.

Quinoa (Keenwah) Fritters: Sounds weird, tastes good! Pan seared cake, slightly nutty in flavor. Served with a green goddess dressing.

Fig and Walnut Bruschetta: A fig, walnut, and brandy spread on toasted bagette and topped with manchego cheese.

Eggplant Fries: Served with pomegranate ketchup.

*Muhummara - Roasted red peppers, walnuts, pomegranate syrup.
*Zatar oil - Sesame seed, fresh thyme, and sun-dried berry infused oil.


Salads

All salads are served on field greens with tomato, carrots, cucumber, shredded parmesan, raisins, and sunflower seeds.
Dressings include: Balsamic, Ginger and Citrus Vinaigrette, Creamy Blue Cheese, and Honey Mustard
Add your choice of meat: Marinated Chicken, Grilled Salmon, Grilled Filet, and Ahi Tuna.


Sandwiches

All sandwiches served with your choice of fries, coleslaw, or grilled maple-butter sweet potato wedges.

House Burger: 8oz burger topped with Boursin cheese, drizzled with an asian ginger-soy sauce, and topped with sautéed onions and mushrooms.

Burger: 8oz burger grilled and served on a fresh Mancini bun.

Salmon Cake: Grilled fresh salmon patty, topped with thinly sliced cucumber and fresh tarragon mayo on toasted ciabatta bread.

Walnut-Arugula Chicken Sandwich: Greek marinated chicken breast, grilled and topped with a walnut arugula pesto, gruyere cheese, and roasted red pepper.

Blackened Swordfish Sandwich: Served with remoulade sauce on a fresh Mancini bun.

Tune Melt: Albacore tuna in a balsamic vinaigrette, smothered with provolone cheese and slices of dill pickle strips.

Hot Peanut Butter and Banana Sandwich: Elvis style and drizzled with honey. Served with vanilla bean ice cream.

Grilled Cheese: Manchego, smoked gouda, and sharp cheddar with oven roasted tomatoes on multigrain bread.

Grilled Vegetable Sandwich: Zucchini, squash, roasted red pepper, portabella mushroom with lettuce, tomato, onion, and sun-dried tomato hummus on toasted multigrain bread.

Grilled Filet Sandwich: Served open-faced on a slice of toasted multigrain bread with sautéed crimini mushrooms and horseradish cream sauce.
